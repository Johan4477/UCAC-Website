About Us

Welcome to the University of Calgary Athletics Club (UCAC), where passion meets performance. Founded as a summer track club for university athletes, UCAC has evolved into a premier organization in Alberta, offering comprehensive training and competition opportunities for athletes of all ages and skill levels.

Our mission is to provide a supportive environment where athletes can achieve personal excellence in athletics. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or new to the sport, there's a place for you at UCAC.

Join us and be part of our journey to success!
